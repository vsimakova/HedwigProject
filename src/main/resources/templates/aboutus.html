☆ What is Hedwig Book Club?

This is a monthly book box that is unique! The coolest team is working on its composing, so you can take my word for it, a product assembled with a soul will come to you!

☆ What is waiting for me in the box in addition to the book?

In addition to the book, 3-4 plot gifts await you. These gifts are somehow interconnected with what is happening, or the heroes use them directly.

☆ How do I know when to open a gift?

On each gift you will see a sticker with the page inscription. As you read through the book, you will find streakers with the words "open the gift". This means that the time has come for him. Find a gift with a matching page number and enjoy.

From time to time, an invitation to a Book Club event will be waiting for you in the box. Let's get acquainted and communicate!

☆ Is it possible to be aware of the name of the book ahead?

The book, like all its components, is a secret until it reaches you. In order to make sure that you have not read it yet, we suggest that you familiarize yourself with the description of the plot in the announcement. This will give a tip and dispel suspicions.
